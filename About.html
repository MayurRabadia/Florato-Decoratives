About Us

Discover Florato Dectoratives!

In the flower-shop sector in Kisumu Florato Decoratives has been operating since 2020 and for 1 year we have continued offering our loyal clients quality and superior service. The founders Ms. Yesha Patel, Ms. Witney Omalla and Mayur Rabadia have more than a year of florist expertise, which is one of Florato Decoratives' strengths as a company, while Mr. Mayur Rabadia is a man of youthful professionalism that helps to grow and expand Florato Decorative Flower shop.

In order to make the operation easy and trouble-friendly, we created a new subsidiary in Garden City, Oginga Odinga Street. As one of the top florists in the Kisumu market, we seek to grow our company with new outlets in other locations nationally to achieve fast and effective service. Florato Decoratives is a prominent florist.

As a pioneer in the flower shop sector, we exclusively provide the best flower in conjunction with the greatest service to meet our clients' requirements. From the beginning our objective is to become the trusted florist to provide for the market of kisumu, thus we've developed an internet presence to potential customers in the Kisumu, to reach prospective consumers all over the world.

Vision

Be the dominant florist in the Philippine business with a broad variety of flowers that are meant to ensure client expectations, bring everyone's grin to their face and make every opportunity unforgettable.

Mission

Our aim is to satisfy the needs of our guests and to create unique flower arrangements for every opportunity to help our guests improve interactions in their life. And to provide our clients the most value for their money by offering high-end floral items at low costs.
